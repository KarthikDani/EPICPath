# MyChart

MyChart is EPIC's patient-facing portal. It allows patients to interact with their health information and communicate with their care team.

## What Patients Can Do in MyChart

- **View test results** -- Lab values, pathology reports, imaging reports
- **Message their provider** -- Secure messaging (goes to provider's InBasket)
- **Schedule appointments** -- Self-scheduling for eligible visit types
- **Request prescription refills** -- Sends a refill request to the pharmacy/provider
- **View visit summaries** -- After Visit Summary (AVS) from recent encounters
- **Pay bills** -- Online bill pay and statement viewing
- **Proxy access** -- Parents can access children's records; caregivers can access dependents

## MyChart and Pathology

Pathology results are released to MyChart based on institutional policy:
- Some hospitals release pathology results immediately
- Others have a delay (e.g., 3-7 days) to give the ordering provider time to discuss with the patient first
- Cancer diagnoses may be held until a provider has contacted the patient
- This is configured per result type in EPIC

## 21st Century Cures Act

The Cures Act (effective April 2021) requires that most test results be made available to patients without delay. This has changed how many hospitals handle pathology result release in MyChart, often leading to immediate or near-immediate availability.

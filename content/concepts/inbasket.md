# InBasket

InBasket is EPIC's internal messaging and notification center. Think of it as your EPIC inbox -- it collects messages, results, tasks, and alerts that need your attention.

## Message Types (Folders)

InBasket organizes messages into folders by type:

- **Results** -- Lab and pathology results that need review
- **Messages** -- Secure messages from other providers or staff
- **Patient Advice Request** -- MyChart messages from patients
- **Rx Requests** -- Prescription refill requests
- **Sign Orders** -- Orders awaiting your signature or co-signature
- **Staff Messages** -- Messages from clinical staff
- **CC Chart** -- You were CC'd on a note or result
- **System Notifications** -- Automated alerts from EPIC

## Managing InBasket

InBasket can become overwhelming quickly. Tips:

- **Dot Phrases in replies** -- Use SmartPhrases to speed up responses
- **Quick Actions** -- Many message types have one-click actions (Approve, Deny, Forward)
- **Pooled InBasket** -- Some messages go to a shared pool (e.g., a lab results pool) rather than an individual
- **Delegation** -- You can delegate your InBasket to a covering provider

## InBasket for Pathologists

Pathologists commonly use InBasket for:
- Receiving amendment requests from clinicians
- Getting notified of add-on test orders
- Reviewing cases forwarded for second opinion
- System alerts for overdue cases or QA flags

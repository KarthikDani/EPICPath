# EPIC Navigation & Common Actions

Navigating EPIC efficiently is one of the biggest learning curves. Here are the essential patterns.

## Finding a Patient

- **Patient Lookup** (Ctrl+1 or magnifying glass icon) -- Search by name, MRN, DOB, or SSN
- **My Patients** list -- Shows patients currently assigned to you
- **Schedule** -- Click a patient from your schedule to open their chart
- **Recent Patients** -- Quick access to patients you've recently viewed

## Essential Keyboard Shortcuts

| Shortcut | Action |
|----------|--------|
| Ctrl+1 | Patient Lookup |
| Ctrl+2 | Schedule |
| Ctrl+3 | InBasket |
| Alt+F4 | Close current activity |
| F6 | Accept/Sign |
| Ctrl+Shift+A | Open a new activity |

Note: Shortcuts vary by organization and configuration.

## Common Actions

### Opening a Patient Chart
1. Ctrl+1 to open Patient Lookup
2. Search by name or MRN
3. Select the patient from results
4. Choose an activity (Chart Review, Notes, Orders, etc.)

### Placing an Order
1. Open the patient chart
2. Go to Order Entry activity
3. Search for the order by name
4. Fill in required fields
5. Sign the order (F6 or Accept button)

### Writing a Note
1. Open the patient chart
2. Go to Notes activity
3. Select note type (Progress Note, H&P, etc.)
4. Use SmartPhrases and templates to build your note
5. Sign and close

### Checking Results
1. Open the patient chart
2. Go to Chart Review or Results Review
3. Filter by date range and result type
4. Click a result to see details

# Results & Chart Review

Once an order is completed, results become available through EPIC's Chart Review activity. This is the primary way clinicians and pathologists view patient data.

## Chart Review

Chart Review is the central hub for reviewing a patient's medical record. It is organized into tabs/sections:

- **Results Review** -- Lab values, pathology reports, radiology reads
- **Notes** -- Progress notes, H&Ps, discharge summaries, operative notes
- **Imaging** -- Radiology images and reports
- **Encounters** -- List of all visits (inpatient, outpatient, ED)
- **Problems** -- Active problem list
- **Medications** -- Current and historical medications
- **Flowsheets** -- Vitals, I&Os, nursing assessments over time

## Viewing Lab Results

Lab results in Chart Review can be displayed as:
- **Table view** -- Results in rows, with columns for date/time
- **Graph view** -- Trend a value (e.g., hemoglobin) over time
- **Cumulative view** -- All results for a given panel grouped together

## Pathology Reports in Chart Review

When a clinician views a pathology result, they see:
- **Diagnosis/Final Diagnosis** -- The pathologist's conclusion
- **Gross Description** -- What the specimen looked like
- **Microscopic Description** -- What was seen under the microscope
- **Synoptic Report** -- Structured cancer reporting (CAP protocol)
- **Addenda/Amendments** -- Any additions or corrections

## Result Notifications

When results are finalized:
- An **InBasket message** is sent to the ordering provider
- Results may trigger **BPA (Best Practice Advisory)** alerts for critical values
- **Abnormal flags** (H, L, Critical) are highlighted visually

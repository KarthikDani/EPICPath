# EPIC Modules

EPIC is a collection of interconnected modules, each serving a specific clinical or administrative function. Here are the major ones:

## Clinical Modules

### EpicCare Ambulatory
Outpatient clinic workflows -- scheduling, check-in, documentation, orders, billing for office visits.

### EpicCare Inpatient
Inpatient hospital workflows -- admissions, rounding, order entry, medication administration, discharge.

### Beaker
Laboratory and anatomic pathology -- accessioning, resulting, pathology sign-out. See the dedicated Beaker concept for details.

### Radiant
Radiology -- imaging orders, reading worklists, report dictation, PACS integration.

### Willow
Pharmacy -- medication dispensing, IV compounding, drug interaction checking, formulary management.

### OpTime
Surgery/Perioperative -- OR scheduling, preference cards, surgical documentation, anesthesia records.

### Stork
Obstetrics -- prenatal care, labor and delivery, fetal monitoring, postpartum.

### Beacon
Oncology -- chemotherapy protocols, treatment plans, infusion management.

## Administrative Modules

### Cadence
Scheduling -- appointment templates, provider schedules, resource management.

### Resolute / Grand Central
Billing and registration -- claims processing, patient registration, insurance verification.

### Cogito
Analytics and reporting -- data warehousing, dashboards, operational reports.

## Communication

### MyChart
Patient-facing portal -- appointment scheduling, messaging, test results, bill pay.

### Secure Chat
Real-time messaging between EPIC users within the same organization.

## Integration

### Bridges
Interface engine for connecting EPIC to external systems (lab instruments, devices, other EHRs).

### Care Everywhere
Health information exchange -- sharing records between different EPIC (and non-EPIC) organizations.
